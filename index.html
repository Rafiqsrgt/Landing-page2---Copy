How does Affiliate Warehouse make money? +

Simple: We only make money when you save money.

When a verified discount is achieved, we retain 1.1% of the savings and you keep the rest (98.9%).

If there's no discount, there's no charge. Fees are billed monthly on verified savings only.

Am I locked in? Can I stop using it anytime? +

No. You can stop anytime.

  • No contracts to sign
  • No minimum spend requirements
  • No exclusivity agreements
  • Cancel anytime, no cancellation fees

If you're not seeing value, you're not obligated to continue. Stop whenever you choose.

How do I know pricing is actually improving? +

You compare side-by-side against your original trade accounts anytime. Our tool shows:

  • Price from your existing account
  • Price through Affiliate Warehouse
  • Your savings (dollar amount and %)
  • Historical pricing trends

No guessing. You control whether you use the pricing or not.

Yes. 100% compliant.

All purchases remain job-coded, fully traceable, and processed through normal wholesaler channels. We operate within your wholesaler's existing terms and conditions.

There's no reselling, circumventing, or bypassing of wholesaler systems. This is collaboration, not disruption.

What if Affiliate Warehouse stops being useful? +

Stop using it. There's no cancellation process, no penalties, no awkward conversations. If you're not saving money, you're not paying.

You're completely in control. Keep your other trade accounts. Use multiple suppliers. You decide.

How are better prices achieved? How can you compete with wholesalers? +

Aggregated purchasing volume. By pooling orders across independent preferred contractors nationally, we reach volume thresholds that typically trigger better pricing tiers from wholesalers.

We're not competing ‚Äî we're working within wholesaler systems to improve volume and consistency, which benefits suppliers too.

Better pricing is achieved through cooperation, not circumvention.

What about my privacy? How is my data used? +

Your data is used solely to contact you about Affiliate Warehouse and to facilitate your price tracking and comparisons.
